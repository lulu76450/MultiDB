Report a mod or a texture pack for DMCA.

We take the respect of copyright laws very seriously. If you notice that a resource on our website infringes upon your intellectual property rights, you may contact us to request its removal.

You can contact us via email at creatif.france@outlook.com. The email must include:

  • Your name and the company or organization holding the copyright for the resource in question;
  • The link to the resource infringing upon your intellectual property rights.

Upon receipt of your request, our team will make every effort to process it within 30 days. If, after this period, the resource infringing upon your intellectual property rights is still available on our website, please follow up with us. Our team consists of volunteers, so it is possible that your request may not have been processed in time.
